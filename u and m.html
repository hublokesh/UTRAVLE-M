Discover Uttarakhand

Hill Stations

1. Nainital – Known for Naini Lake, scenic viewpoints, and temples.

2. Mussoorie – Famous for its colonial charm, Mall Road, and Kempty Falls.

3. Ranikhet – A quiet hill station with beautiful landscapes and temples.

4. Almora – Known for its cultural heritage and Himalayan views.

5. Bhimtal – Lake destination with boating and nature walks.

6. Lansdowne – A serene town with pine forests.

7. Kausani – Offers panoramic views of Himalayan peaks.

8. Mukteshwar – Known for apple orchards and adventure sports.

9. Chopta – Starting point for Tungnath trek, "Mini Switzerland of India."

Pilgrimage Sites

1. Haridwar – Holy city on the Ganges, famous for Ganga Aarti.

2. Rishikesh – Known as the Yoga capital and gateway for Char Dham.

3. Badrinath – Part of the Char Dham pilgrimage in the Garhwal Himalayas.

4. Kedarnath – Known for Kedarnath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas.

5. Gangotri – Sacred place, origin of River Ganga.

6. Yamunotri – Known for the Yamunotri Temple, source of River Yamuna.

7. Hemkund Sahib – Sikh pilgrimage site with a high-altitude lake.

Adventure & Trekking Destinations

1. Auli – A skiing destination with breathtaking Himalayan views.

2. Rishikesh – Famous for river rafting and bungee jumping.

3. Valley of Flowers – UNESCO site with vibrant blooms.

4. Roopkund – Known for the mysterious lake with skeletal remains.

5. Dayara Bugyal – High-altitude meadow trek with scenic views.

6. Pindari Glacier – Trekking destination with majestic glacier views.

7. Nanda Devi National Park – Famous for trekking and biodiversity.

8. Kedarkantha – Winter trek with panoramic mountain views.

9. Nag Tibba – Beginner-friendly trek near Mussoorie.

10. Har Ki Dun – Valley trek with lush landscapes and mountain views.

Wildlife Sanctuaries & National Parks

1. Jim Corbett National Park – Known for tiger reserve and safari.

2. Rajaji National Park – Famous for elephants and diverse wildlife.

3. Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary – Great for bird-watching and Himalayan views.

4. Assan Barrage Bird Sanctuary – Bird-watching paradise for migratory birds.

5. Govind Wildlife Sanctuary – Scenic landscapes and Snow Leopards.

Lakes

1. Naini Lake – Famous lake in Nainital, eye-shaped.

2. Sattal – Group of seven interconnected freshwater lakes.

3. Bhimtal Lake – Large lake with an island in the middle.

4. Naukuchiatal – "Lake of nine corners" with boating activities.

5. Hemkund Lake – High-altitude glacial lake near Hemkund Sahib.

6. Dodital – Freshwater lake popular for trekking and trout fishing.

Unique Places

1. Binsar – Known for its wildlife sanctuary and Himalayan views.

2. Chakrata – Peaceful town, ideal for trekking.

3. Munsiyari – Offers views of Panchachuli peaks, trekking hub.

4. Tehri Dam – One of the tallest dams, popular for water sports.

5. Abbott Mount – Old-world charm and solitude.

6. Kanatal – Secluded hill station with camping and adventure.
